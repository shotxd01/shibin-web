// ShotDevs Startup

ShotDevs is a development service specializing in creating custom-coded solutions for online communities, with a strong focus on the gaming ecosystem. They position themselves as expert partners who collaborate closely with clients to build high-quality, affordable projects.

Our core offerings have expanded to include:

  • Custom Discord Bots: Highly tailored bots with capabilities ranging from simple moderation to complex API integrations.
  • Web Development: Modern, responsive websites and powerful management dashboards for communities and bots.
  • Minecraft Development: A new specialization including the creation of custom plugins for unique gameplay and full server setup and configuration (including BungeeCord/Velocity networks).
